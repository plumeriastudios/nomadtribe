About the Dominican Republic

The Dominican Republic offers a wide range of experiences — from all-inclusive beaches to historic Santo Domingo and inland adventures — making it a flexible choice for new travelers.

Local tip: Bring some small cash in Dominican pesos for local markets and tipping. Many tourist areas accept USD but locals often expect pesos for small purchases.

Important forms & resources

  • Dominican Republic e‑Ticket (official entry form) — complete as required before travel.
  • Go Dominican Republic — official tourism site for attractions and local operators.
  • Viator — recommended for booking tours (check operator reviews).

Top things to do

  • Relax at beaches in Punta Cana or Samaná.
  • Explore Zona Colonial (Santo Domingo) for history and restaurants.
  • Take day trips to El Limón waterfall or Los Haitises National Park.
  • Enjoy local music, dance, and coffee culture.
